## Synopsis

  - Evaluate examples from other Geographies, or from the Japan Geo, on specific engagements to Guide the Conversation.
  - Discuss key Design Considerations that the Tech Seller needs to carefully manage with Clients.
    - How best to Plan, Organize, and Setup for a Proposed PoC.
    - Key architecture decision points that need to be discussed with Clients.
    - Discuss key documentation that should be create to support a Client facing PoC.
    - Calculate the cluster(s) sizing requirements and CP4i licensing entitlement estimates.
    - Key Product Shortcomings that a Tech Seller Should be Aware of and How to Effectively Manage them.
